# Electronics Prohibited Claims Policy

## Prohibited Or High Risk Claims

- Absolute performance claims such as fastest, best, no lag, lifetime battery, or indestructible.
- Unsupported safety claims such as explosion-proof, radiation-free, shockproof, or certified safe.
- Unsupported compatibility claims such as works with every phone, all laptops, or universal for all devices.
- Misleading warranty or service claims not present in structured attributes.
- Use of unrelated brand names for search traffic.

## Audit Action

If the claim is unsupported by product attributes or policy evidence, create a
title_compliance issue. If evidence is insufficient, downgrade the conclusion to
human_review.

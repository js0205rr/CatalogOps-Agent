# Household Title Policy

## Title Requirements

- Identify the main household product type, such as wall art, container, bedsheet, chair, mop, or lamp.
- Include material, size, color, capacity, or dimensions when they affect suitability.
- Do not use unrelated room, lifestyle, or festival keywords if they do not describe the product.
- Do not include promotional claims such as premium, best, cheapest, free shipping, or limited offer unless allowed by policy context.
- For sets and packs, include quantity when available.

## Audit Action

Create title_compliance when the title is too generic, omits the product type, uses
unrelated search terms, or makes unsupported durability and quality claims.

# Electronics Title Policy

## Title Requirements

- The title should identify the actual electronic product type.
- Include brand and model when available.
- Include key specifications that affect buyer choice, such as capacity, connector type, network type, screen size, resolution, or connectivity.
- Do not include unsupported compatibility claims such as "for all phones" unless the description provides evidence.
- Do not include warranty length, certification, or safety claims unless they are backed by seller attributes or policy evidence.
- Avoid promotional wording such as cheapest, best deal, free shipping, limited offer, hot sale, or clearance.

## Audit Action

Create a title_compliance issue when the title is generic, misleading, keyword-stuffed,
or contains unsupported compatibility or performance claims.

# Clothing Prohibited Claims Policy

## High Risk Claims

The following unsupported absolute or exaggerated claims are high risk in clothing
and accessories titles:

- best
- no.1
- number one
- guaranteed cheapest
- 100% guaranteed
- permanent
- never damaged
- miracle
- ultimate
- unbeatable

## Promotional Claims

Promotional claims such as hot sale, clearance, free shipping, discount, cheapest,
lowest price, and best deal should not be used as product-identifying title content.

## Audit Action

If a title contains unsupported absolute or exaggerated claims, create a title_compliance
or title_violation issue, include the matched span, and cite rule or policy evidence.

# Books Title Policy

## Title Requirements

- The title should identify the book or publication being sold.
- Include author, edition, exam, subject, language, or format when relevant.
- Do not include unrelated exam names, authors, publishers, or subjects for search traffic.
- Do not use promotional phrases such as best seller, guaranteed success, free shipping, lowest price, or limited offer.
- Do not imply official endorsement, certification, or guaranteed exam results unless supported by evidence.

## Audit Action

Create title_compliance when a book title contains misleading exam claims, unrelated
keywords, promotional text, or unsupported official/guarantee claims.

# Books Category Mismatch Cases

## High Confidence Mismatch Examples

- A paperback, hardcover, textbook, novel, or study guide is placed under Electronics.
- A book for exam preparation is placed under Household.
- A dictionary, atlas, or encyclopedia is placed under Clothing.
- A religious or devotional book is placed under Household decor when the main item is a publication.

## Human Review Examples

- A stationery set includes a notebook plus non-book accessories.
- A product title mentions a book-like brand name but the description is an electronic device or household product.
- A bundle contains a book and an electronic accessory.

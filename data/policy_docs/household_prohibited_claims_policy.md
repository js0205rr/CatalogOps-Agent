# Household Prohibited Claims Policy

## Prohibited Or High Risk Claims

- Absolute quality claims such as unbreakable, lifetime use, best in world, or permanent.
- Unsupported health or safety claims such as non-toxic, child safe, anti-bacterial, or fireproof.
- Unsupported material claims such as pure wood, stainless steel, organic cotton, or genuine leather.
- Misleading pack quantity, size, or capacity claims.
- Keywords for rooms, festivals, or brands unrelated to the actual product.

## Audit Action

If the product text or attributes do not support the claim, mark title_compliance with
the matched span. If evidence is missing, downgrade to human_review.

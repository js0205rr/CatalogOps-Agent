# Electronics Category Mismatch Cases

## High Confidence Mismatch Examples

- A listing for a Bluetooth earbud or wireless headphone is placed under Household.
- A listing for a smartphone, Android phone, or iPhone is placed under Books or Clothing.
- A memory card, flash drive, or SSD is placed under Books.
- A charger, cable, adapter, or power bank is placed under Household storage or decor.
- A CCTV camera, webcam, or DSLR lens is placed under Mobile Phones when the text does not describe a phone.

## Human Review Examples

- A bundle contains both phone accessories and household mounts.
- The title is only a model number and the description is too short to classify.
- The text contains both product and replacement-part terms without a clear main item.

# Clothing Category Mismatch Cases

## Case 1: Sunglasses Listed As Dresses

Product text contains sunglasses, UV protection, glasses, or eyewear.
Correct category: Clothing & Accessories > Accessories.
If seller category is Dresses, Tops, Bottoms, Bags, or Shoes, mark category_mismatch.

## Case 2: Sneakers Listed As Bags

Product text contains sneakers, shoes, footwear, sandals, boots, slippers, or loafers.
Correct category: Clothing & Accessories > Shoes.
If seller category is Bags, Accessories, Tops, Bottoms, or Dresses, mark category_mismatch.

## Case 3: Wallet Listed As Tops

Product text contains wallet, purse, handbag, backpack, clutch, or tote.
Correct category: Clothing & Accessories > Bags.
If seller category is Tops, Bottoms, Dresses, Shoes, or Innerwear, mark category_mismatch.

## Case 4: T-Shirt Listed As Accessories

Product text contains t-shirt, shirt, tee, top, blouse, hoodie, or sweatshirt.
Correct category: Clothing & Accessories > Tops.
If seller category is Accessories, Bags, Shoes, or Innerwear, mark category_mismatch.

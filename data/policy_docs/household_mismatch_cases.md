# Household Category Mismatch Cases

## High Confidence Mismatch Examples

- A wall painting, framed art, or showpiece is placed under Books.
- A bedsheet, pillow, towel, or curtain is placed under Clothing.
- A kitchen container, cookware item, dinner set, or bottle is placed under Electronics.
- A mop, broom, brush, or cleaning supply is placed under Books.
- A chair, table, rack, shelf, or cabinet is placed under Electronics accessories.

## Human Review Examples

- Smart lights, electronic kitchen scales, and powered appliances may require review between Household and Electronics.
- A product bundle combines home decor with a book or electronic device.

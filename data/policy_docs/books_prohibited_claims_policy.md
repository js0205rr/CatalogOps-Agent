# Books Prohibited Claims Policy

## Prohibited Or High Risk Claims

- Guaranteed exam success, guaranteed marks, or assured selection claims.
- False official, government, board-approved, or university-approved claims without evidence.
- Misleading author, publisher, edition, or language claims.
- Unrelated exam, subject, or author keywords.
- Promotional claims such as cheapest, free delivery, best seller, or limited offer.

## Audit Action

If the claim is unsupported, create title_compliance with matched span and title policy
evidence. If evidence is insufficient, route to human_review.

# Books Category Policy

## Scope

This policy applies to printed books, study guides, reference materials, religious
books, children books, and similar text-based publications.

## Category Routing

- Fiction includes novels, stories, thrillers, romance, fantasy, and literature.
- Non-Fiction includes biography, memoir, business, history, psychology, and self-help.
- Academic & Exam Prep includes textbooks, study guides, question banks, solved papers, and subject exam material.
- Children Books includes picture books, activity books, coloring books, storybooks, and nursery books.
- Reference includes dictionaries, encyclopedias, handbooks, manuals, atlases, and reference guides.
- Religion & Spirituality includes devotional, scripture, meditation, and spirituality books.

## Decision Rule

If a listing is a book but seller_category points to Electronics, Household, or Clothing,
mark category_mismatch with product text evidence. If the listing is a stationery item,
ebook service, or bundled product, send to human_review.

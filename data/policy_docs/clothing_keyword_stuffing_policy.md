# Clothing Keyword Stuffing Policy

Keyword stuffing means adding repeated, unrelated, or search-manipulating words to a
title or description instead of describing the actual product.

## Common Patterns

- Multiple promotional terms in a row, such as hot sale, clearance, discount, free shipping,
  cheapest, and best deal.
- Repeating the same product type without adding useful information.
- Listing unrelated brands, styles, product types, or celebrities to capture search traffic.
- Combining several leaf categories in one title when the item belongs to only one category.

## Examples

- Women dress hot sale clearance free shipping cheapest.
- Nike Adidas Puma style shirt when the item is not related to those brands.
- Shoes shoes shoes best shoes hot sale.

## Audit Action

Mark keyword_stuffing when multiple promotional terms or unrelated search terms appear
together. The seller feedback should ask the merchant to keep only product-identifying
attributes and remove search manipulation terms.

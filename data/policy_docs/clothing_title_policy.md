# Clothing Title Policy

## Title Requirements

- The title should describe the actual product being sold.
- The title should include the product type and useful attributes such as brand, gender,
  size, color, or material when available.
- The title should not include price, shipping terms, delivery date, or time-limited
  promotion.
- The title should not include promotional text such as free shipping, hot sale, clearance,
  discount, cheapest, or best deal.
- The title should avoid excessive punctuation, repeated words, and unrelated search terms.
- The title should not include unrelated brands, celebrities, model names, or product types.

## Audit Action

If the title omits the product subject or contains promotional or unrelated text, create
a title_compliance issue with the matched span and cite title policy evidence.
